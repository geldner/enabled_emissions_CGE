GTAP-E-POWER: extension of the GTAP-E model. GTAP-Power splits electricity sector into 11 generation technologies as well as electricity transmission and distribution.

1.	THEORETICAL STRUCTURE
GTAP-E-Power model is documented in Peters, J., 2016. GTAP-E-Power: An Electricity-detailed Economy-wide Model. Journal of Global Economic Analysis, Vol. 1, No. 2, pp. 156-187. DOI: http://dx.doi.org/10.21642/JGEA.010204AF

2.	DATA BASE
Default.agg from GTAP11A\GTAP-Power\2017
